About

California roots, partnership instinct, and a bias toward building.

I’m a California native, raised in a family of first-generation immigrants, and I’ve spent most of my life here through college, grad school, and now building my career. I took a few detours to New York and Chicago along the way, but California has always pulled me back.

Professionally, I build partnerships that turn emerging technology into real businesses. I’ve worked with teams at Zendesk, Together AI, and Encord to launch AI-powered products, structure marketplace deals, and grow ecosystems from scratch.

I’m especially drawn to helping others navigate the ambiguity of partnerships, whether that means advising founders or sharing what I’ve learned about turning strategy into execution. I’m also deeply interested in how technology can expand human capability.

Outside of work, I’m usually chasing winters, traveling the world with my wife to ski and snowboard, or spending time with my growing family.
